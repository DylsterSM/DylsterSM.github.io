ABOUT

Who is Dylan?

Game Designer, Video Producer, Leader

Where do you see yourself in 15 years?

Working as an Assistant Creative Director or running my own game company.

What is the best part of game development?

Leading and organizing a team to best use every person's strengths to apply a direction and finish a product.

Getting together with friends to play games from the Jackbox Party Pack franchise and laughing for hours and hours into the night!

What do you do for fun?

Hanging out, playing video and board games, and going on trips with friends and family.

What is a fun-fact about you?

I got to meet Lars Gustavsson (former Director, now Producer at DICE) with a friend of mine at EA Play in 2018. I had a great talk with him and shared with him my passions for game development.
